Line

To draw a line, click on any two points on the canvas.

Circle

To draw a circle, first click anywhere for the radius, then click to pick a point on the curve.

Ellipse

To draw an ellipse, first click for two foci, then click to pick a point on the curve.

Rectangle

To draw a rectangle, click points for two opposite vertices.

Polygon

To draw a polygon, click any number of points then click 'Draw Polygon'.

You must click the vertices in order (ie. the vertices will be connected in the order you click.

Polyline

To draw a polyline, click any number of points then click 'Draw Polyline'

You must click the vertices in order (ie. the vertices will be connected in the order you click.
